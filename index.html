Welcome

Playing a game and lost track of scores? No worries, that's what we're here for!

This simple score keeper web app let's you update the scores of two players in a game. All you need to do is :
  • Select the maximum score of the game.

  • Update the score of each player after every win by clicking the score buttons

  • The player who's score turns yellow first, i.e., reaches the maximum score, is the winner.
  • Click RESET for new game.
